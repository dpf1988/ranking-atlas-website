## 4. The case for citation equity

**H2:** Citation equity is the authority signal of the AI search era.

AI systems trust patterns, not single mentions. Omnipresence across authoritative publishers is what creates the pattern. Editorial coverage produces it. Citation equity is the asset that results.

**Pull-quote treatment** (larger type, generous whitespace, visually distinct from body — possibly a left rule or quote mark, treat as a section break not a paragraph):

> **Editorial citations make you a credible source to an AI system. Content volume does not.**

**H3:** Think of it as PageRank for AI.

In classic search, links from trusted sites passed authority and decided rankings. In AI-mediated search, repeated mentions of your brand across trusted publications build the citation profile AI systems rely on when deciding which brands to surface. Same underlying logic. Different mechanism.

We track which domains ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews cite when buyers ask category questions in your vertical. We reverse-engineer the coverage patterns behind the brands AI surfaces. We run campaigns to place you on the same publishers.

## 9. FAQ

**Why does editorial coverage matter for AI visibility?**

AI systems decide which brands to surface based on patterns of mentions across trusted sources. SE Ranking's analysis of 129,000 domains identified the number of referring domains as the single strongest predictor of ChatGPT citations, with the gap between low- and high-citation sites widening sharply at higher coverage levels. The pattern is consistent across studies: brands with broad editorial coverage get cited; brands without it do not.

*Source link: https://www.searchenginejournal.com/new-data-top-factors-influencing-chatgpt-citations/561954/*

**How is this different from SEO?**

SEO optimizes pages on your own domain to rank in Google. Citation equity earns coverage on third-party authority publishers, which is the signal AI systems use to decide who to surface. Seer Interactive's September 2025 study found organic CTR dropped 61% on queries where AI Overviews appeared. Ranking on Google matters less every quarter. Being cited by the publishers AI trusts matters more.

*Source link: https://www.dataslayer.ai/blog/google-ai-overviews-the-end-of-traditional-ctr-and-how-to-adapt-in-2025*

**What's the impact of AI search on traditional traffic?**

It's measurable and worsening. A March 2025 study by Law and Guan found average click-through rate for the #1 Google result fell from 7.3% to 2.6% when an AI Overview appeared, a 34.5% reduction. Digital Content Next's August 2025 publisher data showed median Google referral traffic down 10% year over year, with non-news brands down 14%. The ground is moving. Citation equity is what holds visibility while it does.

*Source links: https://digitalcontentnext.org/blog/2025/05/06/googles-ai-overviews-linked-to-lower-publisher-clicks/ and https://digiday.com/media/google-ai-overviews-linked-to-25-drop-in-publisher-referral-traffic-new-data-shows/*

**Do you guarantee AI citations?**

We guarantee placements on authority publishers. We don't guarantee specific AI citations because no agency that's honest can. AI systems are probabilistic and change with model updates. What we guarantee is the input — the editorial coverage that the data shows is the strongest predictor of AI citation. We measure AI citation outcomes on every campaign so you see what the coverage produces.

**How do you measure AI visibility?**

We baseline your brand's citation rate across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews on category queries before the campaign starts, then measure again after coverage lands. You see which queries you appeared on, which publishers were cited, and how the pattern shifted.

**What happens if you don't hit the placement guarantee?**

We keep working at no additional cost until we do. The campaign isn't complete until your agreed placement count is delivered.

**How long does a campaign take?**

Most campaigns deliver results within 30 to 60 days, depending on the campaign type and vertical. Reactive coverage can land within days when the right news cycle hits. Data-led campaigns and expert commentary typically take longer because they require asset development and targeted pitching.

**What types of publications do you target?**

National news outlets, business and finance press, technology media, and B2B vertical publications relevant to your category. We build a targeted media list for each campaign based on which publishers AI systems already cite in your vertical, plus the publishers your buyers read.

**Do I get to approve angles before they go out?**

Yes. We develop campaign angles in collaboration with you and nothing gets pitched without your sign-off.

**What's included in a campaign?**

Vertical and competitor citation analysis, AI visibility baseline, story angle development, targeted journalist list, pitching and follow-up, placement reporting, and a post-campaign AI citation measurement showing before/after visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and AI Overviews.

**Why no retainers?**

Retainers reward agencies for time spent, not results delivered. Per-campaign means you pay for a defined outcome: a minimum number of placements on authority publishers. If it works, you book another. If it doesn't, you walk away.
